1–2 Year Structured Rotational Program for Fresh Graduates

Overview

MGT Technologies Graduate Program is a prestigious 1–2 year rotational initiative designed exclusively for fresh engineering and technology graduates from premier institutions across India. This program offers structured exposure to the full software development lifecycle in a fast-paced global IT environment.

Participants rotate across high-impact domains, work on live client projects, and receive continuous mentorship from senior leaders — building a strong foundation for a long-term career in technology.

What You'll Experience

Live Projects

Work on real enterprise applications, cloud migrations, AI/ML models, data pipelines, and digital transformation initiatives from day one.

Cross-Domain Rotations

Rotate through 3–4 domains (Software Dev, Cloud, Data/Analytics, Cybersecurity, DevOps) to gain 360° exposure.

Mentorship & Training

One-on-one mentorship, classroom sessions, certifications (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), agile training, and soft skills development.

Career Progression

High-performing graduates transition into full-time roles as Software Engineers, Data Analysts, Cloud Architects, etc. with accelerated growth path.

Who Can Apply?

  • 2024/2025/2026 batch B.E./B.Tech/M.Tech graduates (CS, IT, ECE, AI/ML, Data Science etc.)
  • Minimum 65% or 6.5 CGPA throughout academics
  • Strong programming fundamentals (Java, Python, C++, etc.)
  • Good communication skills and eagerness to learn
  • No active backlogs
