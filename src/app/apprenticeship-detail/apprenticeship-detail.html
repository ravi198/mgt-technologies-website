Program Overview

MGT Technologies Apprenticeship Program is a government-recognized (NAPS eligible) initiative for diploma holders, ITI graduates, and freshers from technical backgrounds. This 6–24 month program combines structured classroom training with real on-the-job experience in a global tech environment.

Apprentices work on live projects, earn a monthly stipend, receive certifications, and build practical skills in IT support, software testing, cloud operations, basic development, hardware maintenance, and digital tools – all under expert mentorship.

Why Join Our Apprenticeship?

Paid Training

Monthly stipend + government incentives (NAPS) while you learn industry skills.

Hands-On Projects

Work on real enterprise tasks – testing, support, cloud setup, and more.

Certifications & Mentorship

Industry-recognized certifications + one-on-one guidance from senior professionals.

Full-Time Job Opportunity

High performers get absorbed into permanent roles with fast career growth.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Diploma (Polytechnic), ITI, or 10+2 with technical background
  • 2023–2026 pass-outs preferred
  • Age: 18–25 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Basic computer knowledge and eagerness to learn
  • No active backlogs
  • Duration: 6–24 months (based on trade & performance)
  • Location: Bengaluru / other MGT locations (hybrid options available)
