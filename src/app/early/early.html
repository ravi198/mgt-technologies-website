Early Careers

Launch your career with purpose, growth, and a supportive team

Joining MGT Technologies means embarking on a journey of continuous learning and growth. Our early career programs are designed to nurture young talent, providing you with the skills and experiences needed to thrive in a dynamic, global environment. Whether you’re looking for internships, apprenticeships, or entry-level positions, we offer a variety of opportunities to kickstart your career.

Our Early Career Offerings.

Internships

Our IT Internship Program at MGT Technologies invites talented students and recent graduates from top engineering colleges, universities, and premier institutions across India for a 4–12 week hands-on experience in real-world software development and technology solutions within a dynamic global tech environment. Interns will gain practical exposure to modern IT practices, including software development, data engineering, cloud technologies, and enterprise applications while working alongside experienced professionals on live projects. This program is designed to help participants build strong technical skills, industry knowledge, and professional experience in a collaborative and innovation-driven environment.

Graduate Programs

Our Graduate Program at MGT Technologies invites fresh engineering and tech graduates from premier institutions across India for a structured 1–2 year rotational experience in a fast-paced global IT environment. Under expert mentorship, participants work on live projects across software development, cloud solutions, data analytics, AI/ML, cybersecurity, and digital transformation; gain hands-on exposure to agile methodologies, client-facing delivery, and emerging technologies; and build core technical, problem-solving, and leadership skills through classroom training, certifications, and cross-functional rotations — paving a clear path to full-time roles as software engineers, analysts, or domain specialists with strong career acceleration in the tech industry.

Apprenticeships

Our Apprenticeship Program at MGT Technologies invites diploma holders, ITI graduates, and freshers from technical backgrounds across India for a 6–24 month blended learning experience combining on-the-job training with classroom sessions in a dynamic global tech environment. Under skilled mentor guidance, apprentices gain practical hands-on exposure in areas like software testing, IT support, cloud operations, basic coding, hardware maintenance, and digital tools; earn a stipend while learning (with government incentives under NAPS where applicable); and develop industry-ready skills through real projects, certifications, and structured assessments — offering a strong pathway to full-time roles in IT operations, support, or entry-level technical positions with excellent career progression in the tech sector.
